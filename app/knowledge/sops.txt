SOP-001: Course Enrollment Procedure
1. Browse the course catalog.
2. Select the desired course.
3. Check the prerequisites.
4. Click 'Enroll'.
5. Wait for manager approval (if required).

SOP-002: Assessment Guidelines
1. Ensure a stable internet connection before starting.
2. Assessments are timed and must be completed in one sitting.
3. A minimum score of 70% is required for passing.
